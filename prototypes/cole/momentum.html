Notification engine + Reality Score

Proactive nudges so Roots never becomes another idea graveyard.

Location-aware and calendar-gap triggers suggest the smallest next action. Every saved item gets a Reality Score—a percentage for how “real-world ready” it is once location, hours, and routing checks land.

Nudge engine (examples)

Push notifications and in-app toasts when you are near a saved stop, when a cluster mate completes a dependency you were waiting on, or when the calendar shows a solvable gap.

📍
Geo nudge

“You are 12 minutes from the lumber yard on your cabinet root—pickup window closes in 45 minutes.”

🗓
Calendar gap

“Saturday 10am–12pm still open—slot ‘rip panels’ from your DIY checklist?”

👥
Cluster unblock

“Leo booked dinner—your Tokyo root is conflict-free. Confirm tempura bar?”

Technical hook: background jobs read device location (with consent), calendar write scope, and push tokens—sketched only in this HTML prototype.

Reality Score

Differentiates Roots from a notes app: each save surfaces a score built from confirmed location, verified hours, transit fit, checklist progress, and group commitments.

92%

9%

Random reel

Fresh save · no ingest run · Reality Score gamifies the next tap

If location, hours, and route checks all pass, the score approaches the high nineties—making “almost real” visible at a glance and nudging users toward the last mile (book, buy, go).
