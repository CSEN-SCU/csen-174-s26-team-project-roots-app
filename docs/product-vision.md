Part 1: Product Vision
1a. Product Vision Statement
FOR People who want to compile and share ideas with their friends on trips, projects, and routes to link between them, but find themselves stuck in inaction from “Doom-Saving.” 
WHO struggle to bridge the gap between digital inspiration (Reels, TikToks) and real-world execution due to the friction of manual planning, research, and compilation of ideas. 
THE Roots App is a Context-Aware Personal Planner THAT automatically transforms saved social media content into actionable calendar events and step-by-step project roadmaps. 
UNLIKE traditional digital calendars or Save folders which act as static archives 
OUR PRODUCT proactively extracts hidden metadata, logistics, and instructional steps from video content to move the user to action sooner and more often. 
POWERED BY Multimodal Content Extraction that identifies locations, dates, and technical instructions from the combined analysis of video, audio, and captions.
1b. Vision Narrative
The Problem in Context: We are constantly fed ideas of projects and trips through social media, yet they often offer little way to work towards the realization of such. We constantly save reels with little to no plan on how to return to them.
Existing solutions: Instagram has built-in location tags and a save feature, but the culture of Instagram urges users to move quickly to the next post. These solutions fall short because locations are unreliably provided by users, and saved posts are hidden behind difficult interfaces. If no information is provided, users have to perform the majority of the work to locate what they want. Since this can be taxing both mentally and physically, most people abandon their ideas at the research phase.
How AI Makes This Possible: Roots uses multimodal content extraction to identify specific locations based not only on Instagram captions or pure text media, but also from information from video clips and audio. These capabilities not only allow roots to identify sites and places when explicit locations are not provided, but also expedite the logistical planning by automatically handling transportation questions and scheduling around business hours. 
The "Without AI" Test:  In a world with no AI, Roots would be a manual input into either a calendar or notes app. You would have to research and write every detail yourself. The value of roots is the efficiency at which a reel can become reality.
